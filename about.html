About Us

Nurturing Leaders of Tomorrow

At Nosegay Convent School, Sikar, we believe education is about shaping character, building confidence, and preparing children for a successful future.

Education with Purpose & Values

Established in 2007, Nosegay Convent School is affiliated with the Rajasthan Board of Secondary Education (RBSE). Located in the peaceful surroundings of Piprali, Sikar, we offer a safe, inspiring space where every child grows academically, emotionally, socially, and morally.

We believe every child carries a unique spark — our role is to nurture that spark into a strong light of knowledge, values, and confidence.
