FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions parents ask about Nosegay School.

What is the admission process for the upcoming session?
The admission process at Nosegay School is simple and transparent. It begins with filling out the inquiry form on our website or visiting the campus. Our admissions team will then schedule a parent-school interaction and a brief assessment for the child. We believe in understanding each child's unique needs before enrollment. For the 2026–27 session, admissions are now open — we encourage you to apply early as seats are limited.
What is the student-to-teacher ratio at Nosegay?
We maintain a low student-to-teacher ratio of 12:1 for our preschool and kindergarten sections. This ensures that every child receives personalized attention, and our educators can tailor their teaching methods to suit each child's learning style. We believe that small class sizes are the foundation of effective early childhood education.
Does Nosegay School offer transportation facilities?
Yes, we provide safe and reliable transportation services for students within a 10 km radius of the school. Our buses are equipped with GPS tracking, seat belts, and trained attendants to ensure the safety and comfort of every child. We also maintain regular communication with parents regarding pick-up and drop-off schedules.
What curriculum does Nosegay School follow?
Nosegay School follows a balanced curriculum that integrates the best practices of the Montessori method, play-based learning, and the RBSE framework. Our approach is holistic — we focus on academic readiness, emotional intelligence, physical development, and creative expression. We also incorporate STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, Mathematics) activities into our daily schedule to nurture curiosity and innovation.
How can I schedule a campus visit?
Scheduling a campus visit is easy! Simply fill out the contact form on this page, or give us a call at +91 90012 34567. Our admissions team will coordinate a convenient time for you to tour the campus, meet our educators, and experience the Nosegay environment firsthand. We host open house events every Saturday from 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM.
